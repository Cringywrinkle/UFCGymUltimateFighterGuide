Shotokan

Overview

In the realm of calculated retaliation, Shotokan shines by combining precise strikes with swift counters, turning enemy aggression against them.

Miscellaneous: Allowed in All-Valley: Yes | Made by: zboys | Style Type: Counter-Offensive

Stats on Moves

  • Punch: Range 4.05 studs, 2.1s CD
  • Right Kick: Range 4.5 studs, 1.4s CD
  • Left Kick: Range 4.5 studs, 1.65s CD
  • Block: 3.5s CD, 0.45s vulnerability
  • Guard Break Duration: 4.5 s
  • Parry Attack Delay: 0.305 s
  • Post Block Attack Delay: 0.7 s
  • Stun Length: 0.74 s
  • Walk Speed: 18.5 studs/s

Pros & Cons

Pros: Balanced strikes, precise counters, excellent footwork, strong punish game.

Cons: Struggles against extreme aggression, lacks high burst damage, can be overwhelmed.

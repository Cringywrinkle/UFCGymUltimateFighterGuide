Street Brawler

Overview

Unrefined yet effective, Street Brawler relies on raw aggression and relentless close-quarters attacks to dominate the chaos of brawls.

Pros & Cons

Pros: High aggression, rapid pressure, excels in close-range combat and corner control.

Cons: Weaker defensive options, vulnerable if attacks miss, limited range.

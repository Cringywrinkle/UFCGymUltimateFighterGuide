Gojo-Ryu

Overview

Melding grace and defense, Gojo-Ryu focuses on fluid movements and strategic blocks, epitomizing the art of deflecting attacks while maintaining a centered stance.

Miscellaneous: Allowed in All-Valley: Yes | Made by: zboys | Style Type: Defensive

Stats on Moves

  • Punch: Range 3.5 studs, 2s CD
  • Right Kick: Range 3.725 studs, 1.25s CD
  • Left Kick: Range 4.65 studs, 2s CD
  • Block: 1.6s CD, 1.35s vulnerability
  • Block Recharge Multiplier: 1.6
  • Delay: 1.8 s
  • Guard Break Duration: 4 s
  • Guard Recovery Delay: 0.45 s
  • Walk Speed: 0.4 studs/s
  • Post Block Attack Delay: 0.51 s (reduced to 0.19 s)
  • Stun Length: 0.9 s

Pros & Cons

Pros: Strong block recovery, excellent counterattacks, punishes aggression, balanced speed.

Cons: Struggles vs. passive foes, less mobility than evasive styles, requires precise timing.

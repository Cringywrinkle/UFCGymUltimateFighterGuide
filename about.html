About UFC Gym

Rank System & Requirements

How Promotions Work: Belt promotions require testing – pass a Belt Test to rank up. Failures remain on hold until you pass. Leadership roles require a 20-round tryout with specific win conditions.

Fighter Progression Ranks

  • Student (0 XP) – Default rank for new members.
  • White Belt Fighter – 1,200 XP + Belt Test
  • Blue Belt Fighter – 3,600 XP + Belt Test (Unlocks Recruiter & Coach Eligibility)
  • Purple Belt Fighter – 7,800 XP + Belt Test (Unlocks Instructor Eligibility)
  • Brown Belt Fighter – 13,500 XP + Belt Test
  • Black Belt Fighter – 20,800 XP + Final Belt Test
  • 7th Degree Coral Belt Fighter – 23,600 XP + Test
  • 8th Degree Coral Belt Fighter – 26,800 XP + Test
  • Red Belt Fighter – 31,000 XP + Test

Competitive Ranks

  • Top 5 Contenders – 500-800 Wins
  • UFC Undisputed Champion – 900+ Wins

Leadership Ranks

  • Recruiter – Blue Belt, 3,600 XP + 20-Round Tryout, Win 10/20 Fights
  • Coach – Blue Belt, 3,600 XP + 20-Round Tryout, Win 12/20 Fights
  • Instructor – Blue Belt, 3,600 XP + 20-Round Tryout, Win 15/20 Fights
  • Head Recruiter – Purple Belt, 7,800 XP + Selection by Management
  • Head Coach – Brown Belt, 13,500 XP + Selection by Management
  • Head Instructor – Brown Belt, 13,500 XP + Selection by Management
  • Chief Recruiting Officer – Black Belt, 20,800 XP + Selection by CEO
  • Chief Coach of UFC Gym – 7th Degree Coral Belt, 23,600 XP + Selection by CEO
  • Chief Instructor of UFC Gym – 7th Degree Coral Belt, 23,600 XP + Selection by CEO

Why This System Works

Skill-Based Progression – Every promotion requires XP, testing, and wins.
Structured Leadership – Clear chain of command.
Competitive & Professional – Only proven fighters earn top ranks.

Staff Responsibilities & Expectations

Lead by Example – Train hard and help others improve.
Maintain Professionalism – Respect all members.
Stay Active – Inactive staff members will be replaced.
Grow the Gym – Contribute by recruiting, training, or managing.
Follow the Chain of Command – Keep the organization disciplined.
